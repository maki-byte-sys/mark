Ingredients

  • Pappardelle pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Procedure

  1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato sauce and season.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce and serve hot.
